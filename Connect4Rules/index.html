Connect 4

Connect 4 is a game between 2 colors/players, pink and green. Each player has a token that they take turns placing that fall to the bottom of the column they are placed in. Once there are 4 tokens of a single color in a row diagonally, horizontally, or vertically, the color of that player will win the game! If all slots are filled, the game will tie and you must restart.

Connect 4 is a 2 player game.
