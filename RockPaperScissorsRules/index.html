Rock, Paper Scissors

A game in which the user makes a choice between rock, paper and scissors. Their selection will then trigger the choice of the computer, and then display the winner and loser based on the selection of both parties. There are multiple ways that either side can win. If one side puts rock and the other puts paper, rock will win. If one side puts paper and the other puts scissors, the side of the scissors will win. And needless to say, if both sides enter the same choice, it will result in a draw.

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a 1 player game; user vs computer
