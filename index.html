Our Games

Connect 4

2 Players

A game between two players where each have a token that are placed in stacks on a board. The object of the game is to get 4 of these tokens in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally before the other player.

Play!

Rock Paper Scissors

1 Player

A game in which the user makes a choice between rock, paper and scissors. Their selection will then trigger the choice of the computer, and then display the winner and loser based on the selection of both parties.

Play!

Tic Tac Toe

1 or 2 Players

A game where the player can choose to play against the computer or another player. The objective is to get 3 in a row on the board.
